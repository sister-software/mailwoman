# Licensing Overview

Whether your business depends on self-hosted geocoding or you just want to use Mailwoman for personal projects, we have a license that fits your needs.

A [**commercial license**](./commercial.md) grants you full ownership and control over your products,
and saves you from having to conform to our open-source requirements and restrictions.

When you purchase a commercial license, you'll also have direct contact with the engineers who built Mailwoman.

An [**open-source license**](./open-source.md) is free to use, but requires you to publish your code under the AGPL-3.0 license. This means that if you use Mailwoman in your own software, you must also make your software open-source under the same license.
